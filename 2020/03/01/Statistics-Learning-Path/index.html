Course: MIT Probabilistic Systems Analysis and Applied Probability(概率分析及应用)

01/03/2020 - 20/03/2020

1 Applied Statistics 2020.03-2020.06

1.1 Overview

1) 探索多元数据集：描述性统计数据和图形显示。多元样本的几何形状。广义方差。协方差矩阵导出的度量。
Exploring a multivariate dataset: descriptive statistics and graphical displays. The geometry of a multivariate sample. Generalized Variance. The metric induced by the covariance matrix.

2) 数据表示和降维：协方差结构分析，主成分分析。独立成分分析。
Data representation and dimensional reduction: the analysis of the covariance structure, principal component analysis. Independent component analysis.

3) 关于平均向量的推论：$Hotelling T^2$检验。置信区域和分量均值的同时比较。Bonferroni方法可进行多次比较。
Inferences about a mean vector:$Hotelling T^2$test. Confidence regions and simultaneous comparisons of component means. The Bonferroni method for multiple comparisons.
家庭错误率和错误发现率。几种多元均值的比较。方差分析和MANOVA。线性模型的推论。
Familywise Error Rate and False Discovery Rate. Comparisons of several multivariate means. ANOVA and MANOVA. Inference for Linear Models.
超越最小二乘法：岭回归，套索，正则化最小二乘。广义线性模型简介。
Beyond Ordinary Least Squares: ridge regression, lasso, regularized least Squares. Introduction to generalized linear models.

4) 区分，分类，聚类。统计分类：模型，分类错误成本和先验概率。
Discrimination, classification, clustering. Statistical classification: model, misclassification costs and prior probability.
贝叶斯监督分类和Fisher方法进行判别分析。
Bayesian supervised classification and the Fisher approach to discriminant analysis.
分类的替代方法：逻辑回归，CART。相似性度量。无监督分类；分层和非分层方法。多维缩放。
Alternative approaches to classification: logistic regression, CART. Similarity measures. Unsupervised classification; hierarchical and nonhierarchical methods. Multidimensional scaling.

5) 统计学习的进展：功能数据分析。数据平滑，降维和表示。功能主成分分析。数据记录：相位和幅度可变性。功能数据分类。
Advances in Statistical Learning: Functional Data Analysis. Data smoothing, dimensional reduction and representation. Functional principal component analysis. Data registration: phase and amplitude variability. Classification of functional data.

6) 统计学习的进展：空间数据。随机字段，方差图模型和方差图拟合。空间预测和Kriging，具有空间依赖性的功能数据。
Advances in Statistical Learning: spatial data. Random fields, variogram models and variogram fitting. Spatial prediction and Kriging, Functional data with spatial dependence.
